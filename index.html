My Favorite Hobby:


Baking


My favorite hobby is baking desserts. During the quarantine for Covid-19 I learned that I enjoyed baking sweets. Baking kept me busy and made sure I wasn't bored while sitting in my house. My favorite recipe is Fudgey Pecan Brownies and Pecan Bread. Pecan Bread is also my family's favorite dessert I make. Baking is entertaining and in the end I get a sweet reward.









My inspiration for baking comes from quaranting for Covid-19. I had a lot of free time on my hands and with the extra time I made many desserts and looked at multiple recipies.
